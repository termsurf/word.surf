You can omit the `-e` on manners if the adverb precedes the verb.
Otherwise if it follows the verb then add it.

| english                             | read                              |
| :---------------------------------- | :-------------------------------- |
| I frequently climb the tree.        | popa frikwante klimi dan dipa.    |
| I climb the tree frequently.        | popa klimi dan dipa frikwante.    |
| This time will soon pass.           | lig kala sun wid pomi.            |
| I straight ran.                     | popa rice sup jari                |
| You are exceptionally good at that. | gona vuti Coz take tavo hatu gula |
